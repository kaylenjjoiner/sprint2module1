Sherrilyn Kenyon (Born December 11, 1985) is an American best selling writer. She was born in Columbus, Georgia as the fourth of five children to parents; Harold and Malene Woodward. She frequently describes her childhood as abuse and poverty filled. She does continue to have a close relationship with her mother who has always shared and encouraged her huge interest in horror, the paranormal, and science fiction.

---Career---

Through out her career Sherrilyn Kenyon has written 102 books since her first book was published in August of 1994. Her latest book will be released in August of this year(2024). Her most popular book series is The Dark Hunter Series, which has 22 books.
